Welcome to Sri Sai Specialty Dental Clinic

Welcome to Sri Sai Speciality Dental Clinic, where expertise meets personalized care. Our clinic is led by Dr. Anitha, owner and chief dentist, with over 30 years of experience in dentistry. Dr. Anitha is a Professor of Pedodontics at a reputed dental college and a graduate of the prestigious Manipal College of Dental Sciences Mangalore. With decades of clinical and academic excellence, she is committed to providing high-quality dental care for both children and adults. At Sri Sai Specialty Dental Clinic, your smile is in expert hands.
